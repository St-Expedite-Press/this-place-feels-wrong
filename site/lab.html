The Compass is not a personality quiz in lighter dress. It is a reading instrument: a way of tracing how you instinctively lean across five metaphysical axes until a historical pattern begins to show itself.

The prompts arrive out of order by design. Answer in one sitting or circle back later; anything you set aside will wait for you, and the reading only opens once the pattern is complete.
