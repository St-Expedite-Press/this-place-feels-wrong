Decision Systems, Ontology Architecture & Auditable AI Infrastructure

St. Expedite Press designs decision intelligence systems for institutions where outcomes must withstand audit, turnover, and time.

We transform ambiguous, high-stakes domains into structured, testable, and version-controlled architectures: ontology-led knowledge graphs, explainable decision logic, auditable AI reasoning systems, and scenario-tested governance frameworks.

Our work integrates semantic data modeling, rule systems, and bounded AI deployment into durable infrastructure, not experimental automation.

Decision Architecture & Governance Systems

We design auditable decision systems for policy, compliance, research governance, procurement, and evaluation contexts.

Every decision artifact is inspectable, contestable, and reproducible, enabling explainable AI deployment, compliance review, and institutional continuity.

  • Requirements capture and constraint formalization
  • Criteria and rubric authoring
  • Decision tables and rule frameworks
  • Exception handling logic
  • Traceability matrices (requirement to rule to output)
  • Governance documentation with version control
Ontology Development & Knowledge Graph Consulting

We build standards-based ontology and semantic infrastructure using RDF, OWL, and SPARQL.

Our ontology systems support multi-stakeholder data integration, longitudinal research environments, regulatory reporting, and institutional memory preservation.

  • Upper, middle, and domain ontology design
  • Taxonomy development and vocabulary governance
  • Knowledge graph modeling and entity resolution
  • Cross-temporal identity mapping
  • Provenance and change-log architecture
  • Interoperable reporting frameworks
Explainable AI & Controlled Agent Systems

We deploy OpenAI models as bounded reasoning components within auditable system architecture. This is controlled AI infrastructure, not chatbot implementation.
