St. Expedite Press builds knowledge systems where decisions must endure.

We convert ambiguous, high-stakes domains into auditable decision logic, ontology-led knowledge graphs, and scenario-tested rule systems.

The emphasis is constraint clarity and chain-of-custody: provenance, traceability, version control, and evidence-linked outputs that remain readable after the moment passes.

We design MCP-style server architectures and multi-agent frameworks that integrate OpenAI models as controlled reasoning layers—with deterministic backstops, evaluation suites, and logs that make failures legible.

Our services are not offered as commodities.
They are offered where seriousness is required.

Knowledge Capture & Requirements

We turn messy domains into shared language and decision-ready structure.

Through interviews, workshops, and terminology alignment, we produce requirements that make constraints explicit, criteria testable, and accountability durable.

  • SME interviews, workshops, and knowledge capture
  • Terminology alignment and definition control
  • Requirements decomposition and decision framing
  • RFP and evaluation-criteria structuring
  • Policy, governance, sustainability, and operations domains
Ontology & Knowledge Graph Modeling

We build layered ontologies (upper / middle / domain) and graphs that support interoperable reporting, constraint-aware reasoning, and identity resolution across time.

Implementation is standards-first: RDF, OWL, SPARQL, explicit vocabularies, and schema governance that survives handoffs.

  • Ontology design, taxonomy development, and term governance
  • Knowledge graph modeling (RDF/OWL) and SPARQL query surfaces
  • Integration patterns for fragmented, multi-stakeholder datasets
  • Cross-temporal identity resolution and interpretive overlays
  • Audit-ready provenance and change logs
Decision Logic & Explainability

We translate policy, governance, and operational constraints into explicit decision artifacts: rules, criteria, and explainable rationales.

The goal is durable decision-making: systems that can be inspected, contested, revised, and re-run without drifting into folklore.

  • Rule and rubric authoring (constraints, criteria, exceptions)
  • Explainable rationales and evidence requirements
  • Decision tables, checklists, and evaluation frameworks
  • Version-controlled governance documentation
  • Stakeholder-readable outputs with technical rigor
Decision QA & Validation

We test decision systems the way you test software: with scenario suites, regression checks, and a disciplined exception catalogue.

When requirements change, you can see exactly what breaks—and why.

  • Scenario suites and regression baselines
  • Edge-case mapping and exception catalogues
  • Traceability matrices (requirement → rule → test)
  • Change impact analysis and release notes
  • Audit-ready validation reports
Agentic Systems & MCP-Style Servers

We design multi-agent systems that keep reasoning bounded: tool-first workflows, deterministic backstops, and evaluation harnesses for controlled deployment.

OpenAI models become accountable components—not opaque oracles.

  • MCP-style server architecture and tool routing
  • Guardrails, policies, and structured outputs
  • Audit logs, provenance capture, and replayability
  • Agent evaluations, red-teaming, and failure modes
  • Hybrid automation: human judgment + machine speed
Data Integration & Knowledge Products

We build pipelines that treat datasets as publications: versioned layers, documented transforms, and stable identifiers that support citation and reuse.

Outputs can ship as datasets, dashboards, dossiers, or hybrid publishing systems combining print, web, and machine-readable artifacts.

  • Python/SQL pipelines; JSONL/CSV/Parquet; raw/derived layers
  • Reproducible builds (Git, Docker) and release workflows
  • Metadata schemas, editorial lineage, and attribution
  • Longitudinal normalization and entity linkage
  • Publishing-grade documentation and distribution
