# Aesthetic Canon

## Palette (Neon Slate)
- Base dark: `#050807`–`#0b0e10`.
- Neon: `#39ff14` (primary wiring), soft: `#a4ffb1`.
- Silt lines: `#161c22`–`#1e252c`.
- Secondary text: `#9fb3a4`.
- No additional colors without approval.

## Typography
- Serif/display: Cormorant Garamond (or Cinzel where present).
- Body: default serif (existing inline styles).
- Keep letterspacing wide on display elements; avoid tight tracking on neon titles.

## Spacing & Layout
- Large negative space; center mass around crest/grid.
- Avoid boxes and cards; rely on text, sigils, and lines.
- Borders: 1px silt; neon glow sparingly.

## Motion
- Allow existing glitch/assemble animations; avoid new heavy motion.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`: ensure key animations disable in CSS/JS.

## Textures
- Maintain grain/scan/mask layers; neon-tint only.
- Background gradients must stay subtle and radial; no hard bands.

## Mood
- Gulf Noir + CCRU: austere, occult-technologic, no SaaS gloss.
- Keep copy minimal, declarative, not chatty.
