# RICE Southern Place Field Notes

This collection applies the RICE **C86 × South × St. Expedite Press** image grammar to New Orleans, Athens, Asheville, Savannah, and Richmond.

Each city receives the same five-image sequence:

1. street field note;
2. working interior;
3. archival evidence;
4. maker portrait;
5. nocturnal aftermath.

The repetition makes the cities comparable through labor, infrastructure, documents, portraiture, and aftermath rather than skyline, tourism, or generic “bohemian” atmosphere.

The complete production prompts, aspect ratios, filenames, shared visual grammar, negative grammar, model, and disclosure language are stored in [`city-image-prompts.json`](city-image-prompts.json).

All generated archival images are visual reconstructions, not authenticated records.
