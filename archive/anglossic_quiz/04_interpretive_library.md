# American Anglossic Compass: Interpretive Library

This document contains the content layer for results: conceptual genealogy, biographical genealogy, and witness excerpts.

## 1. Civic-Sacramental Federalist

Summary:
The world is a hierarchy of beauty; order is the moral imagination made visible.

Conceptual genealogy:
`natural law -> liturgical order -> mixed constitution -> civic reverence -> Civic-Sacramental Federalist`

Biographical genealogy:
`Richard Hooker -> John Adams -> Civic-Sacramental Federalist`

Witness excerpts:

- Richard Hooker, `law as cosmic harmony`: "her voice the harmony of the world."  
  Citation: Richard Hooker, *Of the Lawes of Ecclesiastical Politie*, 1593.
- John Adams, `virtue as the substrate of republics`: "Public Virtue is the only Foundation of Republics."  
  Citation: John Adams, *Letter to Mercy Warren*, 1776.
