This template outlines the structure for a fiction piece on RICE. Like essays, fiction should begin with a header that clarifies the section and volume, followed by the story title and a brief deck or tag line. The byline can include date and location if relevant, though fiction may also omit specific place tags.

The body of the story is presented in the same readable column format as essays. Keep sentences clear and paragraphs distinct. You might include section breaks or ornamental markers between scenes, but avoid ornate graphics. Let the language itself carry the mood.
