RICE / STATEMENT / NEW ORLEANS

The conference is the map. The literature gives the towns their dimensions back.

01 / FINITE

Four issues, then closed.

Seed, Heat, Harvest, Flood. A known end protects the work from becoming a feed or a content treadmill.

02 / REGION

The South is not a backdrop.

It is money, concrete, labor, faith, class, architecture, weather, archive, and systems still operating in the present tense.

03 / METHOD

The SEC is a route, not coverage.

RICE uses sixteen college towns as pressure points. It publishes no rankings, previews, recruiting, or game analysis.

04 / OBJECT

Writers before paper.

The proposed year pays makers, documents rights, establishes sources honestly, and prints an object only when its obligations are funded.
