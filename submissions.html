Submissions

We invite unsolicited submissions of essays, fiction and poetry.

We are especially interested in work rooted in or connected to the Gulf South, but we welcome thoughtful writing from everywhere.

Please send previously unpublished work only. Include a brief cover letter and bio. You can email your submissions to submissions@ricejournal.com. Simultaneous submissions are allowed, but please notify us immediately if your work is accepted elsewhere.

Our response time is normally six weeks. We appreciate your patience and look forward to reading your work.
